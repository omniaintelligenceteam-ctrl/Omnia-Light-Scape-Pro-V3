# Render From Coordinates (Fallback Mode)

- Use normalized coordinates from JSON.
- `xNorm = 0.25, yNorm = 0.70` means 25% from left and 70% from top.
- Place each fixture glow precisely at those locations.
- Do not shift, snap, spread, or rebalance coordinates.
- Every listed coordinate must render exactly one fixture.
- No fixture may appear without a corresponding coordinate entry.
